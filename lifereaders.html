Lifereaders

Lifereaders is a platform where it is possible to have a consultation about your life choices.

They wanted to update their service in order to look fresh and modern, but with interface preservation in order not to lose their old customers.

I increased the significant stuff in a new design, we had images 4 times bigger than previously. I made design lighter visually and fixed the usecases that were too complicated. I also contributed to programming interface with html/css/scss.
